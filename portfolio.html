About Me

Dedicated physiotherapist with specialized expertise in neurological rehabilitation

I am Dr. Sai Charan, a passionate physiotherapist specializing in neurological conditions. With my Master's in Physiotherapy (Neurology), I am committed to helping patients regain their functional abilities and improve their quality of life.

My approach combines evidence-based treatments with personalized care plans tailored to each patient's unique needs. I believe in a holistic approach to rehabilitation that addresses not just physical symptoms but also emotional and psychological well-being.

Fluent in Telugu, English, and Hindi, I ensure clear communication and understanding with all patients throughout their treatment journey.
